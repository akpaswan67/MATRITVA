MAJOR HEALTH CONCERNS

Mental Health

Mental health includes our emotional, psychological, and social well-being. It affects how we think, feel, and act. It also helps determine how we handle stress, relate to others, and make healthy choices.

Climate Change

Climate change increases the risk of illness through increasing temperature, more frequent heavy rains and runoff, and the effects of storms.

Cardiovascular Malfunctioning

Cardiovascular diseases (CVDs) affect your heart and blood vessels. It is the leading cause of death worldwide.

Respiratory Diseases

Respiratory disease are any of the diseases and disorders of the airways and the lungs that affect human respiration.

Cancer

Cancer is a disease caused when cells divide uncontrollably and spread into surrounding tissues. Cancer is caused by changes to DNA.

Covid-19

Coronavirus disease (COVID-19) is an infectious disease caused by the SARS-CoV-2 virus.
